Addition:
   Example:
       0101_2 + 0011_2 = 1000_2 (5 + 3 = 8)

   Subtraction: Use 2's complement of the number to subtract.
   Example:
       0101_2 - 0011_2
       Convert 3 to 2's complement: 0011 → 1's complement = 1100 → Add 1 = 1101
       Now, 0101 + 1101 = 10010 (Drop the carry, result = 0010)
       (5 - 3 = 2)
